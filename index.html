HELLO. WE ARE A PUB. WE LIKE GOOD THINGS. LIKE BEER & PIZZA.

Actually we really, really like good booze. We try very hard to get the absolute best & most exciting modern beers on the bar - in general you'll find a new beer hits the bar every day of the week. We try and avoid big multi-national producers in all our beers, spirits and wines.

We serve food between 12-3 & 5-10pm weekdays, plus we do cooked breakfasts between 10am-2pm Sat & Sun. Some of our food is vegetarian, some vegan, all can be gluten free.

Children are welcome in the pub before 8pm. Drunk kids are really very annoying though, so we probably won't serve them booze. Same applies to dogs.

FURTHER INFORMATION:
We show sports matches on our tv.
Sometimes we have nice people sat in the corner playing pleasant vinyl records.
