The idea will be to provide a parser for text lists of beers. Copy and paste a list of beer names (with the brewery, obv), and it will use data generated by users of online websites such as untappd to return a list of matched beers, in order of popularity (either by number of ratings, or overall rating).
The hope is that it will be useful for guiding where to go at a beer festival, or choosing which beers to buy from a list of available options.
